The Problem

Setting Up a Product Shot Shouldn't Take Hours

Every time you switch objects you redo the same tedious work: move lights, guess camera distance, tweak f-stop, set up the floor — and still end up with inconsistent results across your portfolio.

⚙️

Lights That Don't Scale

A lighting setup sized for a character is blinding on a small prop and too dim on a large vehicle. Every switch means adjusting energy and distances by hand.

📷

Camera Guesswork

Orbiting the viewport, adjusting focal length, moving the camera back — repeated for every object and every angle, never quite the same twice.

🗂️

Batch Renders Are Manual

Rendering 20 assets means 20 manual setups. No way to queue them, no consistent framing or lighting, and no time for anything else while it runs.

🖼️
Screenshot: messy manual viewport — lights scattered, off-center object, inconsistent look

The Solution

Showcase Studio Handles Everything Automatically

1

Lights Scale to Your Object

Every preset measures the object's bounding sphere and sets light energy, size, and distance to match — so a ring and a tank look equally polished.

2

Camera Frames Itself

Choose an angle preset and the camera positions itself at the exact distance for any focal length. Focal length and padding sliders update live — no Apply button.

3

Depth of Field from Object Depth Pro

Pick an intent — Subtle, Dramatic, Extreme Shallow — and the correct f-stop is calculated from the object's actual measured depth. No formulas, no guessing.

4

Batch Renders Unattended Pro

Add objects to the queue and walk away. Each one gets camera framing, lighting, floor, and DOF re-applied before its render fires.
