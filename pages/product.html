Parametric Control

Total Control Over Every Dimension

Adjust cabinet dimensions with precision down to the millimeter. Every parameter is exposed through an intuitive interface that updates your model in real-time.

  • Width, height, and depth controls
  • Panel thickness adjustments
  • Toe kick height and depth
  • Custom gap and spacing values
Drawer System

Unlimited Drawer Configurations

Create any drawer layout imaginable. Add, duplicate, and position drawers independently with full control over size, position, and styling.

  • Multiple drawer instances per cabinet
  • Independent vertical positioning
  • Duplicate with inherited parameters
  • Custom drawer front styling
Non-Destructive

Change Anything, Anytime

Built on Blender's Geometry Nodes system, your cabinet remains fully editable until you choose to finalize it. Make changes at any stage of your project.

  • Full undo/redo support
  • Modify dimensions after creation
  • Convert to mesh when ready
  • Preserves other modifiers in stack
Materials

Production-Ready UV Mapping

Automatic UV coordinates are generated for each surface, ensuring your materials look perfect without manual UV unwrapping.

  • Automatic UV generation
  • One-click material UV setup
  • Works with any PBR material
  • Consistent grain direction
